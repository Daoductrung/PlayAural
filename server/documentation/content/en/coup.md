# Coup

Coup is a game of deduction, deception, and calling your friends' bluffs. The goal is to be the last player standing with influence (face-down character cards).

## Characters
- **Duke:** Takes 3 coins. Can block Foreign Aid.
- **Assassin:** Pays 3 coins to assassinate another player's influence.
- **Captain:** Steals 2 coins from another player. Can block Stealing.
- **Ambassador:** Draws 2 cards and returns 2 to the deck. Can block Stealing.
- **Contessa:** Blocks Assassinations.

## Standard Actions
- **Income:** Take 1 coin. Cannot be blocked.
- **Foreign Aid:** Take 2 coins. Can be blocked by the Duke.
- **Coup:** Pay 7 coins to force a player to lose an influence. Unblockable. Mandatory if you have 10 or more coins.

## Challenges and Blocks
Whenever you claim a character's action or block, any other player can challenge you. You have a few seconds to react.
- If you were telling the truth, you reveal the card, draw a new one, and the challenger loses an influence.
- If you were bluffing, you lose an influence and your action fails.
