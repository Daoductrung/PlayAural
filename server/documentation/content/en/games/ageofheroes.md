Age of Heroes

Age of Heroes is a deep civilization-building card game where players lead ancient tribes to glory. You must manage resources, trade with neighbors, build cities and armies, and wage war to become the dominant empire.

Tribes

Choose from 6 mighty tribes, each with their own special resource needed for their unique monument:

*   **Egyptians** (Limestone)
*   **Romans** (Concrete)
*   **Greeks** (Marble)
*   **Babylonians** (Bricks)
*   **Celts** (Sandstone)
*   **Chinese** (Granite)

Winning Conditions

There are three ways to win the game:

1.  **Empire of Five Cities**: Be the first to build 5 Cities.
2.  **Carriers of Great Culture**: Be the first to complete your Monument by collecting 5 of your tribe's special resource.
3.  **The Most Persistent**: Be the last tribe standing (eliminate all other players).

Gameplay Phases

Each round (Day) consists of several phases:

1.  **Preparation Phase**: Events take effect (e.g., Fortune or Disasters like Earthquake/Eruption are played).
2.  **The Fair (Trading)**: Players can offer cards from their hand to trade with others.
    *   You can request specific resources or accept any offer.
    *   This is crucial for getting the resources you need!
3.  **Play Phase**: Each player takes one Main Action in turn order.

Main Actions

On your turn, you must choose ONE of the following:

**1. Tax Collection**
Draw 2 cards from the deck.

**2. Construction**
Build units or structures if you have the required resources:

*   **Army**: Requires **Iron + 2 Grain**. Used for War.
*   **Fortress**: Requires **Iron + Wood + Stone**. Increases defense.
*   **General**: Requires **Iron + Gold**. Adds bonuses in War.
*   **Road**: Requires **2 Stone**. Connects to a neighbor (requires their permission).
*   **City**: Requires **2 Wood + Stone**. Needed for victory!

**3. War**
Declare war on another player! You can perform one of three types of attacks:

*   **Conquest**: Attempt to seize a City from your opponent.
*   **Plunder**: Attempt to steal cards from your opponent's hand.
*   **Destruction**: Attempt to destroy progress on their Monument.

*Combat Resolution*: Attackers and Defenders commit Armies and Generals. Dice rolls + bonuses determine the winner.

**4. Do Nothing**
Pass your turn.

Special Cards

*   **Resources**: Iron, Wood, Stone, Grain, Gold.
*   **Special Resources**: Tribe-specific (e.g., Marble, Concrete).
*   **Events**:
    *   **Hero**: Can be used as a free Army or General in war.
    *   **Olympics**: Cancels all wars for a round.
    *   **Disasters**: Earthquake (disable armies), Eruption (discard cards).

Keyboard Shortcuts

*   **Roll Dice/Continue**: Follow on-screen prompts.
*   **S**: Check Status (Armies, Cities, etc.).
*   **Shift+S**: Detailed Status.
*   **H**: Check Hand.
