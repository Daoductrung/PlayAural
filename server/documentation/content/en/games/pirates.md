Pirates of the Lost Seas



Access the high seas in this complex RPG adventure! Sail across 4 vast oceans, collect precious gems, battle other pirates, and level up to unlock powerful skills.



Objective



The game ends when all 18 Gems have been collected. The player with the highest score wins!



\* Regular Gems: 1 Point.

\* Rare Gems: 2 Points.

\* Legendary Gem: 3 Points.



Gameplay



Movement \& Map

The world consists of 4 random oceans connected in a line (Total 40 tiles).


\* Level 15+: Unlock moving 2 tiles at once.

\* Level 150+: Unlock moving 3 tiles at once.



Gems \& Leveling

\* Finding Gems: Move onto a tile with a gem to collect it automatically.

\* XP: You gain XP by moving, finding gems, and fighting. Leveled up pirates unlock powerful skills!

\* Golden Moon: Every 3rd round, the Golden Moon rises, tripling all XP gained!



Combat

You can attack other pirates with Cannonballs if they are within range (Default: 5 tiles).

\* Attack/Defense Rolls: Random 1-6 + Skill Bonuses.

\* Hit: You gain XP and can choose to Board the enemy ship.

   \* Push: Shove them 3-8 tiles (Left or Right) to mess up their positioning.

   \* Steal: Roll dice to attempt stealing a random gem from them!



Skills



As you level up, you unlock new abilities:



\* Level 0: Cannonball (Basic Attack).

\* Level 10: Sailor's Instinct (Check map sectors for players/charting).

\* Level 25: Portal (Teleport to an occupied ocean).

\* Level 40: Gem Seeker (Reveal location of a gem).

\* Level 60: Sword Fighter (+4 Attack for 3 turns).

\* Level 75: Push (+3 Defense for 4 turns).

\* Level 90: Skilled Captain (+2 Atk \& +2 Def for 4 turns).

\* Level 125: Battleship (Fire 2 cannonballs in one turn).

\* Level 200: Double Devastation (Increase range to 10 tiles).



Options \& Variants



\* Combat XP Multiplier: Adjust XP gained from fighting.

\* Find Gem XP Multiplier: Adjust XP gained from finding gems.

\* Gem Stealing:

   \* With Bonus: Skill bonuses apply to steal rolls (Default).

   \* No Bonus: Flat dice roll.

   \* Disabled: Stealing is not allowed.



Keyboard Shortcuts



* Up/Down: Navigate menus.

\* Enter: Select action / Fire Cannon.

\* P: Check absolute position.

\* M: Check Moon status (if active).
