Coup

Coup la mot tro choi suy luan, lua doi va vach tran ban be cua ban. Muc tieu la tro thanh nguoi choi cuoi cung con the.

Nhan vat:
- Cong tuoc (Duke): Lay 3 dong. Co the chan Vien tro nuoc ngoai.
- Sat thu (Assassin): Tra 3 dong de am sat the cua nguoi khac.
- Doi truong (Captain): Trom 2 dong cua nguoi khac. Co the chan an trom.
- Dai su (Ambassador): Rut 2 the va tra lai 2 the. Co the chan an trom.
- Nu ba tuoc (Contessa): Chan Am sat.

Hanh dong tieu chuan:
- Thu nhap: Lay 1 dong. Khong the bi chan.
- Vien tro nuoc ngoai: Lay 2 dong. Co the bi Cong tuoc chan.
- Dao chinh (Coup): Tra 7 dong de ep mot nguoi mat the. Khong the chan. Bat buoc neu ban co 10 dong.

Thach thuc va Chan
Bat cu khi nao ban tuyen bo mot hanh dong hay chan, nguoi khac co the thach thuc ban. Ban co vai giay de phan ung.
- Neu ban noi that, ban tiet lo the, rut the moi va nguoi thach thuc mat the.
- Neu ban noi doi, ban mat the va hanh dong cua ban that bai.
